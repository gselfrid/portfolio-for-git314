Projects

Animation

  • TikTok Super Hero

    This first animation follows a super hero character flying and catching a physical TikTok video. This video is meant to portray that TikTok is an easy way to find inspiraiton even when you're a busy bee, such as a super hero! It utilized character appeal and multiple events that happen at once to create a cohesive and eye-cathing animation.

    Click Here to see full animation and full creative brief!
  • One Shoe Skechers

    This next animation had a big focus on the walking animation. It was a practice in trying to have all of the limbs move together to create one realistic visual. It contrasted heavily with the life-like bird that's animated simply, showing that even with just one Skeckers shoe, you can hold your ground against nature. It also utilizes text animation for a pleasant ending screen.

    Click Here to see full animation and full creative brief!
  • Snowman-Kicking Ninja

    This animation displays a ninja jumping up and coming down to smash a snowman's head. I wanted to see if I could create the illusion of a lot of movement without using that much actual movement in the animation. I had to utilize perspective in the character design as well as unity in the limbs' movements to create the illusion of the ninja jumping down.
